TNCs Today: what is this?

You're looking at an estimate of the number of TNC (Uber and Lyft) pickups and dropoffs in San Francisco—by location and by time of day.
  • Uber and Lyft trips are combined.
  • Only rides that are entirely within the city limits are counted.
  • Darker colors and higher bars mean more trips to/from an area.
  • Data is averaged from several weeks in fall 2016.
  • No personal data is shown. Pickup/dropoff locations are merged by city block downtown, and by larger areas farther out.
  • Every effort has been made to ensure this data is accurate, but SFCTA cannot verify its correctness without outside assistance.
